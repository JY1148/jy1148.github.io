Personal Talk

The Outland of logic and sensation

Stress

It is unavoidable to say that it is my keynote of the recent life. For Five months, now. Anxiety and Pressure. It comes upon every moment I am sober (Sometimes it disguised as nightmare). The internship is really hard to find. The timing is hard, and as an individual, I have to brave this situation with the same body, using of which I enjoyed joy of life.

I told myself, and Julay, one year ago that the following period would be the hardest time for us ever. I thought it would be a time for me to grow with adversity. But the truth is a time to suffer with unexpected psychological negative mood. I even felt the depression came. And fortunately went away. But not rainy does not equalize sunny. Cloudy always.

Is there any good news?

I have to say that I grew up a lot. Now my body can suffer more anxiety. Even though this is simply triggered by a really small thing: searching internship. But this shallow life experience really gives out deep agony, which can also prove that I was not strong enough to better handle such negligible stuff.

Starting from scratch, I learned programming in Java, Python and JavaScript in school and beyond campus(Thank you, the Internet). I could be the emblem of best utilizing the web to learn knowledge. I have a strange conviction that I must have better learning strategy and skill than others because I taught how to learn.

I built projects. Watching videos and following step by step. Every time I had an aha moment paves my way to progress. I really love aha moment. I really really enjoy having aha moment. It triggers dopamine. It makes me happy.

Now I have a relatively good understading of full stack. From database to API, from CRUD to routing system, from auth and authz to web deployment, I enjoy learning new things. I hope this would continue until I am strong enough.

Staying negative

Honestly, I dont like this murmuring. I stay outgoing in my image most of the time. And I must be super embarrassed in the future when I turned back and read this shit. I hope life could be simpler, more foci on development and creating new value.

That is how I work. That is how life works. Wish you Good Luck! Joe.

Jinzhou Yan
